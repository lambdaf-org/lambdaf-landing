§03 The bench rules

How they're built

Where a tool makes a claim or touches your data, these hold.

01 Receipts
A claim about a person, a relationship, or the law carries a quote and a pointer back to the source, or it does not get written down.
02 Computed confidence
Confidence is computed from counted evidence and shown with its tally, never asserted.
03 Explicit about what leaves
Your files stay on disk. The tools that reach a model strip secrets first and say what goes out.
04 No ceremony
Several tools are one Markdown protocol with the model as the engine. Nothing to deploy, no schema to babysit.
